Ühe luuletuse lehekülg

Sellele lehele oli soovituslik üles laadida enda CV või portfoolio. Kuid maailmas on juba üsna palju tõsiseid ja tähtsaid lehti, seega sellel siin on hoopiski:

  • üks luuletus,
  • üks pilt seda luuletust saatmas.

The sky fell down
and in my ball gown
I rushed to the stars
I gazed upon Mars
		
The stars held me tight
pulled closer in the night
so welcoming their beams
were to fulfil my dreams
		
as among them I danced
something made me glance
a rope tied to a tree
the other end holding me
 
although I lacked gravity
I was bound to reality
This is how I feel –
weightless in between
Luuletus on pärit 2019. aastast.
Brigitta Sari
